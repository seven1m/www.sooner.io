Sooner.io

Sooner.io is the distributed job scheduling engine and web-based management app built on Node.js and Hook.io, brought to you from the Sooner State.

Job List

Manage many jobs all from a single interface, and distribute their load across any number of machines on the network.

Job Management

Create, edit, and schedule jobs from the web interface. Write your job code in CoffeeScript!

Job Monitoring

Monitor output of your job in real-time, whether or not it succeeds.

Status Page

See the connectedness of various nodes in the system and messages being sent via Hook.io.
